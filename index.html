About

Hi, I'm Valerie. I'm a non-traditional student nearing the end of my final year at the University of Montana. I started as a Studio Arts major, but switched to Media Arts to pursue animation. I've been having a great time learning the cutting edge 3D animation and modeling techniques and hope to het into virtual reality. However, I'm also hugely inspired by old-fashioned stop motion animation and have loved learing the techniques and traditions. I hope to contribute to keeping the art alive, continuing to build upon its rich heritage as a weird, charming, and creepy medium.
